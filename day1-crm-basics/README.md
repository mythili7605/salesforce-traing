  # Day 1 - CRM Basics

## 1. What is CRM

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It is a system used by companies to manage customer data, sales, communication, and support in one place.

Example:
Companies use CRM to store customer phone numbers, emails, purchases, and interactions.

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

Salesforce helps companies to:

- Manage customers easily
- Track sales
- Improve communication
- Automate tasks
- Increase business growth

Salesforce is cloud-based, so it can be accessed from anywhere.

---

## 3. Explanation

### Account
An Account represents a company or customer organization.

Example:
Infosys, Amazon, TCS

### Contact
A Contact is a person associated with an Account.

Example:
Employee or customer details like name, phone number, and email.

### Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential sale or business deal.

Example:
A company planning to buy software from another company.

---

## 4. Real-World Mapping

Example:

- Account → College
- Contact → Student
- Opportunity → Student placement opportunity

Another Example:

- Account → Amazon
- Contact → Manager at Amazon
- Opportunity → New software purchase deal
